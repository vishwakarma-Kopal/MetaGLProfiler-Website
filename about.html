About us

    1. Introduction

MetaGl Plofiler, an interactive approach for browsing profiles. Instead of traditional method of presenting resume and portfolios, people can share their profiles in innovative manner. Combination of Metaverse+WebGL+AWS brings cloud to project. In a world where first impressions matter, MetaGL Profiler offers a unique path for individuals to present their skills and accomplishments. It goes beyond the static nature of traditional resumes, providing a dynamic and interactive platform for both job seekers and recruiters alike.

    2. Mission

• Our Project revolutionize the way individuals engage with resumes by providing a dynamic, immersive, and visually appealing experience within the metaverse.
• We used AWS service which is S3 which ensures a robust, scalable, and secure deployment of MetaGL Profiler on the Amazon Web Services (AWS) cloud infrastructure.
• Our objective is to create a dynamic space for professionals to connect, collaborate, and build meaningful relationships and MetaGL Profiler facilitates real-time interactions, fostering networking opportunities within the metaverse.
• Prioritize user experience by designing an intuitive and user-friendly interface.
Our goal is to make MetaGL Profiler accessible to a wide range of users, providing a seamless and enjoyable experience for both navigating resumes and engaging in multiplayer interactions.
• Our objective is to bridge the gap between physical and virtual networking. MetaGL Profiler seeks to create a platform where professional connections transcend geographical boundaries, offering users a unique opportunity to build relationships in a borderless metaverse.
